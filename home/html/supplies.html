Emergency Supplies

Supplies Expiry Check

-- days

Water & Food

  • Bottled water - 1 gallon/person/day
  • Canned food (beans, tuna, vegetables)
  • Dry biscuits & protein bars
  • Manual can opener
  • Baby food & formula (if needed)

First Aid Kit

  • Prescription medications (2-week supply)
  • Bandages, gauze, antiseptic
  • Pain relievers & fever reducers
  • Masks & gloves
  • Thermometer & scissors

Tools & Equipment

  • Flashlight + extra batteries
  • Battery-powered radio
  • Power bank (portable charger)
  • Duct tape & scissors
  • Whistle for signaling

Documents & Money

  • ID copies (passport, driver's license)
  • Insurance policies
  • Birth certificates
  • Cash (small bills & coins)
  • Spare house & car keys

Sanitation

  • Hand sanitizer & soap
  • Toilet paper & wet wipes
  • Garbage bags & plastic ties
  • Disinfectant spray
  • Feminine hygiene products

Clothing & Shelter

  • Change of clothes (warm & dry)
  • Sturdy shoes & raincoat
  • Blankets & sleeping bags
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Emergency poncho
